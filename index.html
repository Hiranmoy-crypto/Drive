Contacts Us

A tree plantation and picnic program was organized with teachers, students, and other community members, making it a delightful blend of environmental responsibility and recreation. The day began with planting saplings, where everyone actively participated, learning the importance of trees in maintaining ecological balance. After the plantation drive, a picnic followed in the lush green surroundings. Teachers and students enjoyed delicious food, sang songs, played games, and shared stories, creating a joyful and memorable experience. The program combined learning, bonding, and entertainment, leaving everyone with a sense of accomplishment and happiness.
